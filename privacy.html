KeyFix – Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 10, 2025

No Data Collection

KeyFix does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All text processing happens entirely on your device.

Accessibility Access

KeyFix requires Accessibility permission to monitor keystrokes and convert text between keyboard layouts. This data never leaves your device.

Analytics

We do not use any analytics, tracking, or crash reporting services.

Contact

If you have any questions, please visit our support page.
